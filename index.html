ABOUT ME

Coding is my Passion

I'm a second-year B.S. Computer Science student studying at the University of California - San Diego. I recently found my love for coding when I discovered how limitless the possibilities with it were. Since then, I’ve learned languages such as Python, Java, and C and key concepts which include algorithms and data structures. Java is the language I’m most skilled in, but I’m willing to learn any programming language that suits the needs of my employer. I strive to be someone that is continually learning new concepts and improving upon the knowledge I have gained. This push for obtaining new knowledge can be seen in my desire to learn elements of web development. I’m constantly looking for ways to gather new knowledge and get constructive feedback on the work that I’ve already done.
